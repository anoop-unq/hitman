The Origins

King Shantanu marries Ganga; Bhishma is born. Shantanu's marriage to Satyavati leads to Bhishma's vow of celibacy. Birth of Pandu, Dhritarashtra, and Vidura.

🕑

The Childhood of Kauravas and Pandavas.

Pandu and Dhritarashtra's children grow up together. Rivalry begins as Duryodhana harbors hatred for the Pandavas.

☮

Pandavas in Exile

Pandavas lose their kingdom in a dice game with Shakuni. Thirteen years of exile, including one year of incognito living.

⤈

The Kurukshetra War

The epic battle lasts for 18 days. Key events include the Bhagavad Gita discourse, the fall of Bhishma, and Drona’s demise.

㉨

Aftermath and the Rule of Dharma

Pandavas regain the throne and rule justly. The death of Krishna and the onset of Kali Yuga mark the end of the epic.
